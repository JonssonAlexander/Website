Tracking China's second-hand residential market between June 2024 - September 2025.

  • General intuition: The nationwide policy Sep 2024 temporarily remidied the second-hand housing market.
  • Harbin stands alone with roughly 10 million residents and a 48.5‑month de-stocking cycle versus the 32-city average of 14.8 months.
  • Anything > 36 months signals severe inventory pressure, highlighting how overbuilt Harbin remains.
  • Excluding Harbin, this policy briefly relieved stock across metros covering ~688 million of 700 million urban residents before the momentum faded.
